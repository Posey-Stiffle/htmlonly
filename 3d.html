I made my scene feel ethereal by using light colors and making my scene bright. I made the shapes of everyday objects irregular to make it dreamlike. I want my scene to invoke a sense of wonder and whimsy with the choice of my colors and how otherworldly the monitor, book, and cup are. I added mountains floating on clouds in the background to not only fill in the composition but also to add to the ethereal feeling within this piece.

Every 3D model within this virtual space is created by me. This world is meant to instill upon the viewer a feeling of being placed among miniature figures. The user is very tall compared to the colorful alphabet/numerical creatures on the ground. The water and the floating cloud islands make the place feel ethereal and otherworldly, since they are assets I had created for the previous desk project. As the user walks around, they may come across images referencing pop culture with accompanying sound effects. The music that plays in the background makes the atmosphere feel playful.
