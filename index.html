>>> BLENDER <<<<

I have been using Blender since 2019 and I can model characters, backgrounds, props, rig and animate. I find it that I don't have a real limitation with 3D modeling like I do with 2D illustrations. If I really try hard enough, I can make a decent looking model of anything!

[STATUS: ACTIVE]

>>> ANIMATION <<<<

I have been making animations since 2016. I started making animations on my 3Ds and made animation memes all until 2019, 2020. I stopped making them because I realized I had a bigger passion for story telling. Which is possible with animation memes but it takes a massive effort to make a story with them. It's easier to forgo the bopping to head animations and move onto different mediums.

[STATUS: EVOLVING]

>>> ILLUSTRATION <<<<

I have been drawing since 2016. Or at least, that's the year where I actually started taking it seriously. I draw animals mostly and I use Sketchbook for my digital illustrations. However, I have been starting to use Photoshop more and more as I have access to it now through SJSU.

[STATUS: ALPHA]

>>> STORYTELLING <<<<

Story telling is the biggest passion I have in life. When I was younger, I thought I liked drawing and animation the mist but as I got older I realized why I liked it so much is not because of the act of drawing or the end result of the piece, but for the story it told. Anything can have a story, even a black dot on a white canvas.
